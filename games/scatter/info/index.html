Info on Scatter

Scatter was published by wooden toy company Montgomery Schoolhouse in the 70s. Their lineup was mostly pure toys like cars and trains, but included the odd game such as this one:

Tic tac whatnow?Photo by Infinity Creations

Montgomery schoolhouse are still around today, as part of Maple Landmark. Their webshop is well worth a visit if you enjoy wood- and love-based products!

There is very little known about Scatter beyond the rules. We've reached out for more information, and will update this article if we get it!

Scatter is a simple race to be the first to scatter your pieces across the board. What makes it interesting is that one of the 9 board quadrants are missing, and instead of moving you can slide another quadrant into the hole.

Chessicals can't replicate this exactly like that since our boards are never stateful, so we have replaced the hole with a troop of neutral units working in exactly the same way.
