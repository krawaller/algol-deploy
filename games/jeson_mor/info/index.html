Info on Jeson Mor

Jeson Mor! A traditional mongol game where riders fight for the center. The name means "nine horses" in Mongolian.

Mongol riding warriorImage by Biopharma Reporter

Don't be fooled by the simple rules, the game is very hard to master. Calculating many steps ahead is key, with little room for errors.
