Info on Serauqs

Serauqs was published in 1981 by Competitive Games, without designer credit.

The Competitive Games editionPhoto by Thomas MacLaney

In Serauqs players compete to be the first to form a winning pattern. The main gimmick is that one of your pieces also matches for your opponent, allowing for surprise wins.
