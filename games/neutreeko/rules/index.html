Neutreeko rules

Neutreeko is played on one of the following boards and setups:

Regular Neutreeko to the left, small variant in the middle and mini variant to the right.

Players take turn to one of their orthodiagonally, as far as it can in that direction without leaving the board or entering/jumping over an occupied cell.

Win by arranging your into a line! Diagonals included.
