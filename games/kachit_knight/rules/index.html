Kachit Knight rules

Kachit Knight is played on a 4×4 board with the following setup:

There are 5 unit types in the game:

  • Orthogonal knight , move like chess rook
  • Diagonal knight , move like chess bishop
  • Unpromoted leader , move like chess king
  • Orthogonal leader , move like king or rook
  • Diagonal leader , move like king or bishop

All pieces capture by replacement, like in chess.

Players take turn to either:

  • Drop one of their 3 off-board knights on an empty square in the castle by their throne, as either or
  • with unpromoted leader . If reaches grass field, promote to or
  • Move knight or promoted leader, afterwards selecting or orientation for next turn

There are two win conditions:

  • regicide - Kill the enemy leader
  • infiltration - Move your leader to enemy throne
