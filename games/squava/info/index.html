Info on Squava

Squava was invented by Néstor Romeral Andrés in 2011 as a simplification of Yavalath, the famous first invented-by-a-computer-program game.

Playable by blind people!Pic by NestorGames

Squava takes Yavalath's board, shrinks it and uses square cells instead of hexes. The result is of course a simpler game, but that also means it is more accessible. And, somehow it gets a certain flair of its own!
