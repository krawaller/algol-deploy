Mad Bishops rules

Mad Bishops is played on a 10×10 board with the following setup:

Players take turn to move a . They move and capture like chess bishops, but with two restrictions:

  • if a can kill an enemy, it must do so
  • otherwise it must move to a position from which it can kill next turn

First player to annihilate the other wins!
