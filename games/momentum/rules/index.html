Momentum rules

Momentum is played on an initially empty 7×7 board. Players take turn to a onto the board.

After the first by Player 1, Player 2 can choose to take over the placed .

Afterwards the momentum of the dropped unit travels outwards in all 8 directions, going through adjacent units, and the last dropped unit in each line will be pushed.

If after dropping you have all 8 out on the board you immediately win!

Draws are not possible.
