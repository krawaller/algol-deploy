Daggers rules

Daggers is played on a 8×8 board with the following setup:

Players take turn to move one of their units.

  • A moves and captures like a chess king.
  • A moves in any direction except east-west. When heading "uphill" (northwards) they can only move 1 step, "downhill" (southwards) they move any number of steps. They capture enemies, except for other orthogonally.

There are two ways to win:

  • infiltration: move a into the opponent base
  • regicide: kill an opponent

Draws are not possible.
