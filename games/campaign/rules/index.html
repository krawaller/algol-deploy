Campaign rules

Campaign is played on an initially empty 10×10 board.

For their first turn players will each a anywhere on the board (except Player 1 may not use the 4 central squares).

Then they will take turns to their like chess knights onto an empty square, spawning a in the vacated square.

Win by:

  • winline: Form a line in any direction of 4 or more
  • starvation: Block in the opponent
